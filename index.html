Construction of Campus

BBOR has no permanent campus to carry out our growing programs and activities and keep up with the increasing need in our communities. We currently rent our campus that includes 2 buildings which provides year round shelter to 110 girl orphans and vulnerable children and their caretakers. Rentals in Zambia, although the economy is declining,continue to rise. In order to maximize our donations, we have created a long term plan to purchase 10 acres of land and plan to construct more suited living quarters, dining room and other facilities needed to care properly and comfortably for our deserving children. This will also enable us to open our doors and offer a safe haven to more orphaned and vulnerable children.
